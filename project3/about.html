Alpine Survival

High Concept

While avoiding trees, animals, and other snowboarders, the player must maneuver their way down the hill at barreling high speeds. Power-ups and coins are scatterd along the mountain to move through obstacles are add to the score.

Genre

Action/Survival

Platform

Desktop only

Story

The snowboarder wants to reach the bottom of the mountain in one piece. It is a fast-paced descent that leaves the player excited and nervous. The player wants to reach the bottom with maximum health while collecting coins for to achieve a high score.

Aesthetics

Graphics: Cartoonish
Sound: 8-bit and ambient
Types of Sounds:

  • Background Music
  • Collisions
  • Level Complete

Gameplay

Mechanics
Move side to side avoiding obstacles and collecting coins and power-ups.

Controls
Keyboard

Teaching the Game
Controls will be displayed either to the side of or below the game screen. Items and obstacles will be displayed in a similar fashion.
